WORK & PROJECTS

a run-through of my work experience and personal projects

SAP Labs
Software Engineer: 5.16 - Present | Project Manager Intern: 6.17 - Present

Starting in May 2016, I have worked as a software engineer intern for SAP Labs in Palo Alto helping to develop and update the QA team's Java-based, automated testing framework. To aid in the testing of SAP's BI Information Steward web application, I have also expanded the team's framework using Apache JMeter, which allows me to build framework at a much faster rate and speed up individual testcases by up to 50%.

UC Santa Barbara Research Mentorship Program
Student Researcher | 6.14 - 8.14

For this project, I worked with Anthropology PhD Sangin Kim at UCSB and used object-oriented programming and agent-based simulation with R to model group selection by dispersion and cultural processes. In this model, there were positive and negative influencers that would interact with each other via a point based system throughout their lifetime and have offspring who would continue this process for multiple generations. The applications of this study reflect ideal group sizes given certain starting parameters for work, study, and more!
