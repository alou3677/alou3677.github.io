what's my hype?

Fashion Footwear: BOOST

In the summer of 2016, my friend introduced me to the world of hype-fashion sneakers during the big Adidas NMD drop in June. After I had copped my first pair of NMD_R1 Reflectives, my afinity for 'athleisure' sportswear has exploded and left me with my own personal collection of NMDs, UltraBoosts, and Yeezys alongside a great portfolio of sneaker photography as well!

Automotive Culture: VOLVO

Mom's 2002 Volvo S40, my high school 2012 Volvo S60 T5, and now a 2015 Volvo S60 T5 AWD. I think it's safe to say that Volvo has been in my life for a very, very long time. While I'm passionate about all sorts of cars from dirty-road rally legends like the Delta S4 to the track-ready McLaren 675LTs and P1 GTRs, Volvos have always had a special place in my heart.

Competitive Swimming

I've been swimming for over 80% of my life, and of that about half has been dedicated to swimming competitively on my year-round club team, the California Dolphin Swim Team based out of Fremont, CA. In high school, I may have taken swimmiing more seriously than my actual studies, but I learned a great deal from the pool and did end up with Sectionals cuts (bonus pts for you if you know what this means) for my best sprint freestyle events.

Downhill Skiing

Snow, speed, and scenery. I'm not sure what else you could be asking for in the winter season, but that's is all I need to enjoy the mountains.
